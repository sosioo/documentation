Accounts

#Account backup

Back-up your data locally and in the cloud.
All post and messages are backed-up Followed users and following users are backed-up.

#Account recreation

Re-follow all or part of followed users automatically from your new account. Re-post all or partof your post automatically from yournew account.

https://lifehacker.com/whats-in-your-instagram-data-dump-and-how-to-get-it-1825777655

#Activities are back (if you want to)

IG has removed the following tab. But some users want their followers to be able to see which posts they likes or which person they follow.

#The activity tab

Show what you want to show and who can see it.

Actions you can show or hide in your activity tab:
Likes, Follow, Comments

You can mark some profile so any action in relation with that profile does not appear in your activity tab. The activity tab can ask confirmation before add the activity.
